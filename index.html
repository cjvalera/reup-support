Private by design

Your data stays yours

No servers we control. No analytics SDKs. No third-party trackers. Just your inventory, on your devices.

On-device first

All data is stored locally with Apple's SwiftData. The app works fully offline.

Optional iCloud

If you turn on sync, your data goes to your personal iCloud — not ours. Your iPhone and iPad stay in step.

No tracking

No analytics, no advertising IDs, no telemetry. Read the privacy policy.

What it does

Built for clinical reality

Every bag tracked from delivery to administration with batch-level precision.

Batch & lot tracking

Record lot numbers and expiration dates per batch. ReUp uses First-Expired-First-Out so the right bag is always next.

Expiry & low-stock alerts

Configurable warning windows surface expiring batches and items running low — before they affect a visit.

One-tap treatments

Define a treatment once, then apply it to a client to decrement every component in the right order — with undo.

Volume tracking

For multi-dose vials, track partial use down to the milliliter. Open vials are drawn from first.

Appointments & reminders

Schedule one-off and recurring visits with address autocomplete and on-device notification reminders.

Full history & export

Every change is logged. Export inventory, batches, and history as CSV — or generate a PDF report for the chart.
