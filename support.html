Common Questions

How do I get started?
When you first open ReUp, the onboarding flow will guide you through setting up categories and adding your first inventory items. You can also import items from a CSV file.

Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is also backed up to your personal iCloud account. We never have access to your data.

How do I restore my data on a new device?
Sign in with the same Apple ID on your new device and install ReUp. Your data will sync automatically from iCloud.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Open an issue on our GitHub Issues page. Please include your iOS version and device model for bug reports. We also welcome feature requests and suggestions.
