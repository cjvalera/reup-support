Privacy Policy

ReUp - Inventory Tracking for Mobile IV Nurses

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Overview

ReUp is designed with your privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data.

Data Storage

All data you enter into ReUp — including inventory items, appointments, treatments, and usage history — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This data is associated with your Apple ID and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data We Do Not Collect

ReUp does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Device identifiers or advertising data
  • Health or medical information

Third-Party Services

ReUp uses the following Apple services, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy:

  • iCloud (CloudKit) — optional data sync between your devices, stored in your personal iCloud account
  • MapKit — address autocomplete and directions for appointments, processed by Apple
  • Notifications — local appointment reminders scheduled on your device (not sent through any server)

ReUp contains no third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, or advertising libraries.

Data Export

You can export your inventory, batch, and history data as CSV files, and your history as a PDF report, directly from the app's Settings. Exported files remain on your device unless you choose to share them.

Data Deletion

You can delete any data within the app at any time. Uninstalling ReUp removes all local data. To remove iCloud data, delete ReUp's data from your iCloud account settings.

Children's Privacy

ReUp is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.
